How to Use the Character Counter Tool

  1. Type or paste your text in the text area above.
  2. The tool will automatically count characters, words, lines, letters, and numbers.
  3. Use the "Copy Text" button to copy your text to the clipboard.
  4. Use the "Clear" button to clear the text area and start over.

About Character Counter

Our Character Counter tool provides accurate counts of characters (with and without spaces), words, lines, letters, and numbers in your text. This is useful for:

  • Social media posts with character limits (Twitter, Instagram, etc.)
  • SEO meta descriptions and titles
  • SMS messages with character restrictions
  • Writing assignments with specific character requirements
  • Data entry and form validation
