About YouTube Thumbnails

YouTube thumbnails are preview images that represent videos on the platform. They play a crucial role in attracting viewers to click on and watch videos. YouTube provides thumbnails in several resolutions:

  • Maximum Resolution (maxresdefault.jpg): 1280×720 pixels - The highest quality thumbnail available.
  • High Quality (hqdefault.jpg): 480×360 pixels - Standard high-quality thumbnail.
  • Medium Quality (mqdefault.jpg): 320×180 pixels - Medium quality thumbnail.
  • Standard Definition (sddefault.jpg): 640×480 pixels - Standard definition thumbnail.
  • Default (default.jpg): 120×90 pixels - The smallest thumbnail size.
Not all resolutions may be available for every video. The maximum resolution thumbnail is only available for videos uploaded in HD.
