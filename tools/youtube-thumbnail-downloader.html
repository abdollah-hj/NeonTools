About YouTube Thumbnails

YouTube thumbnails are the preview images that represent videos on the platform. They play a crucial role in attracting viewers to click on and watch videos. YouTube provides several thumbnail resolutions:

  • Default (120x90): The smallest thumbnail size.
  • Medium Quality (320x180): Medium-sized thumbnail.
  • High Quality (480x360): Higher quality thumbnail.
  • Standard Definition (640x480): Standard definition thumbnail.
  • Maximum Resolution (1280x720): The highest quality thumbnail available, usually in HD.
  • Maximum Resolution (1920x1080): Full HD thumbnail, if available.
This tool works with public YouTube videos only. Private or unlisted videos may not work properly.
