How to Use the Word Counter Tool

  1. Type or paste your text in the text area above.
  2. The tool will automatically count words, characters, sentences, and paragraphs.
  3. Use the "Copy Text" button to copy your text to the clipboard.
  4. Use the "Clear" button to clear the text area and start over.

About Word Counter

Our Word Counter tool provides accurate counts of words, characters (with and without spaces), sentences, and paragraphs in your text. It also estimates the reading time based on an average reading speed of 200 words per minute.

This tool is useful for:

  • Students writing essays with word count requirements
  • Content writers creating articles of specific lengths
  • Social media managers ensuring posts fit character limits
  • Anyone who needs to analyze text statistics
