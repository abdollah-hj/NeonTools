How to Use the Instagram Photo Downloader

  1. Open Instagram in your browser or app and navigate to the post you want to download.
  2. Copy the URL of the post from the address bar (or use the "Share" > "Copy Link" option in the app).
  3. Paste the URL in the input field above.
  4. Click the "Get Media" button.
  5. Once the media loads, click the "Download Media" button to save it to your device.

About Instagram Media

Instagram hosts various types of media content:

  • Photos: Single image posts.
  • Videos: Video content up to 60 seconds (feed posts) or longer (IGTV).
  • Carousels: Multiple photos or videos in a single post that users can swipe through.
  • Stories: Temporary content that disappears after 24 hours (not supported by this tool).
  • Reels: Short-form videos similar to TikTok.
This tool works with public Instagram posts only. Private posts cannot be accessed.
