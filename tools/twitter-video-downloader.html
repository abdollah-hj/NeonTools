How to Use the Twitter Video Downloader

  1. Find a tweet containing a video that you want to download.
  2. Click on the tweet to open it in a separate page.
  3. Copy the URL from your browser's address bar.
  4. Paste the URL in the input field above.
  5. Click the "Get Video" button.
  6. Once the video loads, select your preferred quality and click "Download" to save it to your device.

About Twitter Videos

Twitter supports various video formats and qualities:

  • Standard Videos: Regular videos uploaded directly to Twitter.
  • GIFs: Animated GIFs that play automatically in the timeline.
  • Live Videos: Streams that can be recorded and replayed after the broadcast ends.
  • Video Quality: Twitter typically provides videos in multiple qualities (low, medium, high).
This tool works with public Twitter posts only. Protected tweets cannot be accessed.
