How to Use the Facebook Video Downloader

  1. Find a Facebook post containing a video that you want to download.
  2. Click on the video to open it in a separate page or right-click and select "Copy video URL".
  3. Paste the URL in the input field above.
  4. Click the "Get Video" button.
  5. Once the video loads, select your preferred quality and click "Download" to save it to your device.

About Facebook Videos

Facebook hosts various types of video content:

  • Regular Videos: Videos uploaded directly to Facebook by users or pages.
  • Live Videos: Streams that can be recorded and replayed after the broadcast ends.
  • Stories: Temporary videos that disappear after 24 hours (not supported by this tool).
  • Reels: Short-form videos similar to TikTok.
  • Watch Videos: Longer-form content from creators and publishers.
This tool works with public Facebook videos only. Private videos cannot be accessed.
