How to Use the Case Converter Tool

  1. Type or paste your text in the text area above.
  2. Click one of the case conversion buttons to transform your text.
  3. Use the "Copy Text" button to copy the converted text to your clipboard.
  4. Use the "Clear" button to clear the text area and start over.

About Case Converter

Our Case Converter tool allows you to quickly transform your text into different cases:

  • UPPERCASE: Converts all characters to uppercase (ALL CAPS)
  • lowercase: Converts all characters to lowercase
  • Title Case: Capitalizes the first letter of each word
  • Sentence case: Capitalizes the first letter of each sentence
  • Capitalize Each Word: Capitalizes the first letter of each word (similar to Title Case but stricter)
  • aLtErNaTiNg CaSe: Alternates between lowercase and uppercase letters
  • InVeRsE cAsE: Inverts the case of each letter (uppercase becomes lowercase and vice versa)
  • RaNdOm CaSe: Randomly assigns uppercase or lowercase to each letter
