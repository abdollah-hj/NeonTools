How to Use the TikTok Video Downloader

  1. Open the TikTok app or website and find the video you want to download.
  2. Tap the "Share" button and select "Copy Link" (in the app) or copy the URL from your browser's address bar.
  3. Paste the URL in the input field above.
  4. Click the "Get Video" button.
  5. Once the video loads, click "Download Without Watermark" to save the video without the TikTok watermark.

About TikTok Videos

TikTok is a popular social media platform for creating and sharing short-form videos. Key points about TikTok videos:

  • Video Length: TikTok videos can be up to 10 minutes long, though most are shorter.
  • Watermark: TikTok adds a watermark with the creator's username that bounces around the video.
  • Video Quality: TikTok videos are typically available in multiple qualities, with the highest being 1080p.
  • Music and Sounds: TikTok videos often include music or sound effects that are part of the video file.
This tool works with public TikTok videos only. Private videos cannot be accessed.
